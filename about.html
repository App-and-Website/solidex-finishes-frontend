About Us

At SolidEx Finishes, we take pride in being Sydney’s trusted epoxy flooring specialists, delivering quality, precision, and innovation in every project. With over 15 years of experience, our team combines technical expertise and a passion for excellence to create durable, safe, and visually stunning floors for industrial, commercial, and residential spaces.

Introduction to Solidex Finishes

SolidEx Finishes is a trusted name in epoxy flooring, proudly serving Sydney and surrounding regions with premium-quality flooring solutions. We specialise in designing, installing, and maintaining high-performance epoxy floors for industrial, commercial, and residential spaces. With over 15 years of experience, our team has developed a deep understanding of the diverse requirements across various industries, allowing us to deliver tailored flooring systems that combine durability, functionality, and aesthetic appeal.

At SolidEx Finishes, we are driven by a commitment to excellence, safety, and long-term performance. Using Australian-certified materials and the latest installation techniques, we ensure every project is completed to the highest standards. We take pride in transforming ordinary floors into durable, seamless, and visually striking surfaces that stand the test of time.

We are known as Best Epoxy Flooring Company in Sydney

Commitment to Quality, Safety & Compliance

At SolidEx Finishes, quality and safety are at the core of everything we do. We adhere to strict Australian Standards to ensure every epoxy flooring system we install meets the highest levels of durability, performance, and workmanship. Our experienced team follows a meticulous process from surface preparation to final coating ensuring flawless finishes that are not only visually appealing but also built to last in demanding environments.

Our Mission

To deliver high-quality, durable, and visually striking epoxy flooring solutions with a strong focus on safety, innovation, and customer satisfaction, transforming every space with precision and care.

Our Vision

To be Sydney’s most trusted epoxy flooring company, recognized for exceptional quality, reliability, and sustainable flooring solutions that stand the test of time.

Tailored Solutions

Every floor is unique and so are your needs. We offer customised epoxy flooring solutions for homes, garages, warehouses, and commercial facilities, ensuring the perfect balance of style, safety, and strength.

Experienced & Certified Professionals

Reliable Service & Lasting Results

From consultation to installation, we ensure a seamless experience with clear communication and dependable timelines. Our commitment doesn’t end at project completion, we stand by the quality of every floor we create.
